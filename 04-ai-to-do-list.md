Simple Daily To-Do List for a Class 12 Student

Morning

- ☐ Wake up on time.
- ☐ Freshen up and eat a healthy breakfast.
- ☐ Spend 10–15 minutes reviewing yesterday's notes.

School / Classes

- ☐ Pay attention in class.
- ☐ Take clear notes.
- ☐ Ask questions if something is unclear.

Afternoon

- ☐ Take a short break after school.
- ☐ Complete homework.
- ☐ Study one difficult subject for 45–60 minutes.
- ☐ Take a 10-minute break.

Evening

- ☐ Revise another subject for 30–45 minutes.
- ☐ Practice sample questions or numericals.
- ☐ Pack your school bag and prepare for the next day.

Night

- ☐ Review what you learned today for 10–15 minutes.
- ☐ Make a short to-do list for tomorrow.
- ☐ Sleep for 7–8 hours.
